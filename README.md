## Description

This application allows users to create, store, and update their very favorite recipes. It was created to be a virtual cookbook to keep all recipes in one place so the user never loses a go to recipe again.

## Usage

To open the deployed application in your browser, click [here](#). You will be directed to the homepage which prompts you to sign up or login. <br> <br> <img src="./public/images/screenshot1.png" style="border: 1px solid blue;" width=100% /> <br> <br> After logging in, the user will see a welcome page with instructions on how to use the app: <br> <br> <img src="./public/images/screenshot2.png" style="border: 1px solid blue;" width=100% /> <br> <br> Initally, your recipe book will be empty but you can add recipes by clicking on the "Add Recipe" button in on the page or by clicking the link in the navbar. <br> <br> <img src="./public/images/screenshot3.png" style="border: 1px solid blue;" width=100% /> <br> <br> <br> <br> <img src="./public/images/screenshot4.png" style="border: 1px solid blue;" width=100% /> <br> <br> After you've added some recipes, you'll see recipe cards in your recipe book. <br> <br> <img src="./public/images/screenshot5.png" style="border: 1px solid blue;" width=100% /> <br> <br> You can click on the recipe name or you can use the search bar to search by recipe name using the autocomplete feature to select your desired recipe. <br> <br> <img src="./public/images/screenshot6.png" style="border: 1px solid blue;" width=100% /> <br> <br> <br> <br> <img src="./public/images/screenshot7.png" style="border: 1px solid blue;" width=100% /> <br> <br> Once viewing the full recipe card, you can click on edit to update or delete your recipe. <br> <br> <img src="./public/images/screenshot8.png" style="border: 1px solid blue;" width=100% /> <br> <br>
